Pastor's Message

City of Praise

It is with great joy and overflowing excitement that, I welcome you specially to the official website of City of Praise Zone. This church is a perfectly prepared place by the most High God for imperfect people like you and I . Here in City of Praise, our expectations are always turned around to glorious manifestations, by our All-Able God. We have a track record of the supernatural God working behind the scenes to answer our prayers and, also fight every battle that confronts us. Therefore I welcome you to this prepared home of Winners, Conquerors and Overcomers.

Here at City of Praise, we are committed to excellence in ministry and to minister to the total man, woman and children. We believe in an all round spiritual and physical development, so that you can dominate in all circumstances and situations of life. Life is a gift, and I dare you to live before you leave.

Blessings and Love,

Pastor Andrew Eborka (Zonal Pastor)
